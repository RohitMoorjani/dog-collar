what is woof?

Woof is an initiative to prevent painful deaths of stray dogs at night. We provide neon collar badges free of cost to the stray dogs of your society. This helps drivers at night spot the stray dog well before the impact preventing an innocent animal's untimely death.

how does it work?

You let us know the requirement of a neon collar in the area by volunteering with us. The collar would be sent to your area and voila you're a superhero for a super cute doggo :)

how can you help?

become our friendly neighbourhood spiderman :)

We alone cannot reach every area in our city, but together we can! Just count the number of stray dogs in your locality, add the necessary details by clicking on the button given below and the collars would be provided to them by us.

help us buy more collars

Every collar costs around 20rs. Every penny received will be contributed to saving lives of these innocent souls :)
